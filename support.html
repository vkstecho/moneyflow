Frequently Asked Questions

How does auto-tracking from SMS work?
When you grant SMS permission, MoneyFlow reads only bank transaction messages (not personal chats). It extracts the amount, merchant, and date, then automatically categorizes the expense. Everything happens on your phone — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
Is my SMS data sent to any server?
No. All SMS processing happens entirely on your device. We never upload, store, or transmit your messages. Not even to our own servers.
Which banks are supported?
MoneyFlow supports SMS from all major Indian banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB, BOB, Equitas, Canara, Union, IDBI, Yes Bank, Federal, IndusInd, RBL. It also supports UPI apps like Paytm, PhonePe, GPay, CRED, and Slice.
Can I use the app without SMS permission?
Yes! SMS permission is completely optional. You can add transactions manually, import from Axio PDF exports, or set up recurring bills. The app works fully in manual mode.
What are Need, Want, and Savings categories?
Every expense is tagged as Need (essentials like food, rent, bills), Want (lifestyle like shopping, entertainment), or Savings (investments). This helps you understand your spending patterns using the 50/30/20 budgeting rule.
How do I export my transaction history?
Go to Settings → "Export Statement (PDF)". This generates a branded PDF report with all your transactions, category breakdown, and Need/Want/Savings analysis.
How do I import data from Axio or other apps?
Go to Settings → "Import from Axio PDF". Select the PDF statement exported from Axio. MoneyFlow will parse all transactions and auto-categorize them.
What happens if I uninstall the app?
All your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all transaction history, budgets, and settings. We recommend exporting a PDF statement before uninstalling.
A transaction was categorized incorrectly. Can I fix it?
Currently you can delete the incorrect transaction and re-add it manually with the correct category. We're working on an edit feature for a future update.
Is MoneyFlow free?
Yes, MoneyFlow is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges.

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open MoneyFlow → Settings → "Clear All Data" to remove all local data

2. Uninstall the app to remove all remaining data from your device

3. To delete your Firebase authentication record, email us at vksnitian@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered phone number
