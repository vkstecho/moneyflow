Privacy Policy

1. Overview

MoneyFlow ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance tracking application developed and operated by VKS Tech. This privacy policy explains how MoneyFlow collects, uses, and protects your information.

🔒 MoneyFlow is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your financial data stays on your device. We do not upload, sell, or share your SMS data or transaction history with any third party.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Phone Number (for Authentication)

We collect your phone number solely for login verification via Firebase Authentication (Google). An OTP is sent to verify your identity. We do not use your phone number for marketing, sharing with third parties, or any purpose other than authentication.

2.2 SMS Messages (for Transaction Tracking)

⚠️ SMS Permission Disclosure (Google Play Requirement)

MoneyFlow requests the READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions to provide its core functionality: automatic expense and income tracking from bank transaction SMS messages.

What we read:

We ReadWe Do NOT Read
Bank transaction SMS (debit/credit alerts)Personal messages from contacts
UPI payment confirmationsOTP messages from other apps
Credit card transaction alertsPromotional/marketing SMS
Wallet notifications (Paytm, PhonePe, etc.)Social media notifications

How SMS data is processed:

  • SMS messages are filtered on-device to identify only bank/financial messages
  • Transaction details (amount, merchant, date, account) are extracted on-device
  • The parsed data is stored locally on your device only
  • Raw SMS content is never stored, uploaded, or transmitted to any server
  • SMS processing happens entirely on your phone — no cloud processing

✅ SMS data NEVER leaves your device. All parsing and storage happens locally. We have zero access to your messages.

2.3 Financial Data (User-Created)

When you manually add transactions, budgets, splits, or recurring bills, this data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. This data is not uploaded to any server.

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ✗ We do NOT collect your name, email, or address
  • ✗ We do NOT collect your bank account numbers or card numbers
  • ✗ We do NOT collect your location data
  • ✗ We do NOT upload your SMS to any server
  • ✗ We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • ✗ We do NOT use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • ✗ We do NOT serve ads or share data with advertisers

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 On-Device Storage

All financial data (transactions, budgets, splits, recurring bills) is stored locally on your device using Android's secure application storage (AsyncStorage). This data is sandboxed — other apps cannot access it.

4.2 Firebase Authentication

We use Google Firebase Authentication for phone number verification. Firebase stores your phone number on Google's secure servers solely for authentication purposes. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.3 No Cloud Sync

MoneyFlow does not sync your data to any cloud server. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your transaction history will be permanently deleted from the device.

5. SMS Permission Justification

MoneyFlow's core functionality is automatic expense tracking from bank SMS messages. This is the primary and only reason we request SMS permissions.

Why SMS permission is necessary:

  • READ_SMS — To scan existing bank messages and import past transactions when the user first enables the feature
  • RECEIVE_SMS — To detect new bank transaction messages in real-time and automatically log them as they arrive

How we ensure responsible use:

  • Users must explicitly grant permission — it's not required to use the app
  • Only messages matching bank/financial patterns are processed
  • Personal messages are completely ignored and never read
  • All processing happens on-device — no server communication
  • Users can revoke permission at any time from Android Settings
  • The app functions normally without SMS permission (manual entry mode)

8. Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke SMS Permission — Go to Android Settings → Apps → MoneyFlow → Permissions → disable SMS. The app will continue to work in manual mode.
  • Delete All Data — Use the "Clear All Data" button in the app's Settings screen to permanently delete all transactions and financial data.
  • Delete Account — Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. To delete your Firebase authentication record, contact us at the email below.
  • Export Data — Use the "Export Statement" feature to download a PDF of your transaction history before deleting.

9. Children's Privacy

MoneyFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Data Retention

  • On-device data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Firebase auth data: Retained until account deletion is requested
  • SMS data: Raw SMS content is never stored. Only parsed transaction details are kept on-device.
