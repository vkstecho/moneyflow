Account & Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You have full control over your data. Follow these steps to completely remove your account and all associated information.

1
Clear App Data
Open MoneyFlow → Settings → tap "Clear All Data". This permanently deletes all transactions, budgets, splits, and recurring bills from your device.
2
Uninstall the App
Uninstall MoneyFlow from your phone. This removes all remaining local data, cached files, and app preferences.
3
Request Authentication Deletion
To remove your phone number from Firebase Authentication, send us an email with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered phone number.
📧 Send Deletion Request

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your transaction history will be lost. We recommend exporting a PDF statement from the app before deleting.

What Gets Deleted

✓
Local Data (Immediate)
All transactions, budgets, splits, recurring bills, and settings are deleted immediately when you clear app data or uninstall.
✓
Authentication Record (Within 48 hours)
Your phone number will be removed from Firebase Authentication within 48 hours of receiving your deletion request email.
✓
SMS Data
MoneyFlow never stores or uploads raw SMS data. Parsed transaction data is stored only on your device and is removed with uninstallation.
